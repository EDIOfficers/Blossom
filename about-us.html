Our Mission

Creating a Space for Healing

Blossom was created with a single purpose: to provide a safe, supportive, and personalized digital companion for those navigating the complex journey after miscarriage.

We believe that healing requires both emotional support and practical resources, delivered with compassion and understanding.

Every feature of Blossom has been carefully designed to address the unique needs that arise after pregnancy loss. We recognize that this experience affects each person differently, which is why our platform offers personalized support through mood tracking, journaling, resource connections, and gentle guidance.

By combining evidence-based approaches with a human-centered design, Blossom aims to reduce feelings of isolation and provide tools that empower users throughout their healing journey.

Our Story

Blossom began in 2023 when our founder, Sarah, experienced a miscarriage and found herself struggling to find digital resources that provided both emotional support and practical guidance in one place.

October 2023

Initial concept developed based on interviews with 30+ individuals who had experienced pregnancy loss.

January 2024

Multidisciplinary team assembled, including healthcare professionals, mental health specialists, and technology experts.

March 2024

First prototype developed and tested with a focus group of 15 individuals with lived experience.

June 2024

Beta version launched with improved features based on user feedback. Partnership established with three NHS trusts.

February 2025

Blossom 1.0 officially launched with comprehensive tracking tools, NHS integration, and personalized support resources.

Today, Blossom continues to evolve based on user feedback and emerging research, ensuring we provide the most effective and compassionate support possible.
